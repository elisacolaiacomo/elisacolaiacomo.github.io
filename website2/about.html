A bit more about me...

Born and raised in Rome. Food lover, travel addict.

Currently enrolled in the Msc in Inequalities and Social Sciences at the LSE.

Prior to joining the LSE I completed a Ba in International Economics, Management and Finance at Bocconi University. While majoring in Economics, disciplines such as Development Economics and Political Economics brought me closer to the notion of Inequality.

As such, I decided to join the MSc in Inequalities and Social Science to deepen my understanding of this complex concept encompassing many diverse fields. I am mainly interested in educational and economic inequalities, and their far-reaching societal consequences.

I particularly love that the MSc is highly multidisciplinary, while still allowing me to tailor the study of inequality to my main area of interest.
